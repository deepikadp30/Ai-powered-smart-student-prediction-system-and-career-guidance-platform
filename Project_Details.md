AI-Powered Smart Student Prediction System and Career Guidance Platform

Project Overview

The AI-Powered Smart Student Prediction System and Career Guidance Platform is an intelligent web application designed to predict student academic performance and provide personalized career guidance using Machine Learning techniques.

The system helps educational institutions identify students who may require additional academic support and assists students in making informed career decisions based on their academic performance, skills, and interests.

---

Objectives

- Predict student academic performance using Machine Learning.
- Identify students at academic risk.
- Provide personalized career recommendations.
- Improve student success and decision-making.
- Support teachers, counsellors, and administrators with data-driven insights.
